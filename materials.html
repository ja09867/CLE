How It Works

1. Laser Module

The laser module emits a focused light beam that engraves or cuts the surface of your chosen material. Power and focus define engraving quality and depth.

2. Stepper Motors

Stepper motors move the laser along the X, Y, and Z axes with precision. This controlled movement ensures accurate engraving paths.

3. Control Board

The control board interprets commands from software and controls the laser and motors. It’s the main brain of the entire machine.

4. Software

Software like LaserGRBL or LightBurn converts images or text into G-code. This G-code tells the board how to move the laser for engraving.

5. Power Supply

The power supply ensures the laser and motors receive the correct voltage and current to run smoothly and safely.
